Our Services

ELO Petroleum is a high effeciency and production service company that supports global
oil and gas companies. This includes the transportation of Petroleum and gas to refineries, and
the processing and delivery of energy assets to market. ELO Petroleum has two areas of
operations. These are; drilling, completions and production services and mining services. we
also aid businesses to make better use of rich pools of data and digital technologies which
increases theuse of predictive maintenance, automated pipeline inspections and deep analysis
of wells, among many other uses.

We also support companies to establish an integrated operations strategy, transform faster without massive organisational upheaval, and manage complex mergers and acquisitions more effectively.

We support companies and organisations in

  • Drilling, completion, and production
  • Legal and political consultancy
  • Procurement and Logistics
  • Project and strategic Management
  • Operation Management
  • Risk Management
  • E-commerce and marketing
